Taylor Swift Blog

Being a college student means balancing your social life, academics, and even trying to be well rested for the next day. Though school can be consuming, it is also important to invest in hobbies and self care. Some things I like to do to destress include: cooking, reading, hanging out with friends and family, and listening to music. One of my hobbies also includes knowing everything about Taylor Swift and her discography.

Taylor Swift is one of the most well-known singers of the 21st century. Starting her career in country music, Swift has now taken over the globe with 10 albums, all with different styles and aesthetics, or as she calls them Eras. At the beginning of last year, Swift started The Eras Tour after having not toured since she went on tour for the Reputation album. She wanted this tour to be quite different, especially after releasing two new albums and rereleasing her old albums. Taylor Swift is known for her poetic mastery while detailing stories of her life. Fans around the world adore her and her work.
